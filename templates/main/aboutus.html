About FWL

Empowering women to live stronger, healthier and more confident lives.

Meet Louise

Hi, I'm Louise — a certified personal trainer and fitness coach with over 10 years of experience helping both men and women transform their lives through movement, nutrition and mindset.

My journey into fitness began as a passion and quickly became my purpose. Having worked with hundreds of women across all fitness levels, I understand that every body is different and every goal is personal. Whether you're just starting out or looking to take your training to the next level, I'm here to guide and support you every step of the way.

At FWL — Fitness With Lou — my mission is simple: to create a welcoming, inclusive and empowering fitness community where every woman feels confident, strong and inspired to be her best self.
